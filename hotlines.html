Hotlines & Services

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

24/7 confidential support for those in crisis.

Call: 988

Crisis Text Line

Text-based support for any crisis, available 24/7.

Text: 741741

SAMHSA National Helpline

Free treatment referral and information service.

Call: 1-800-662-HELP (4357)

National Domestic Violence Hotline

Support for those experiencing domestic violence.

Call: 1-800-799-SAFE (7233)

Trevor Project

Crisis support for LGBTQ+ youth.

Call: 1-866-488-7386

Veterans Crisis Line

Confidential support for veterans and their families.

Call: 988, then press 1
