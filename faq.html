Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mindful Check-In?
Mindful Check-In is a platform dedicated to mental well-being, offering premium yoga, meditation, and psychology courses, along with a supportive community to help you achieve mindfulness and balance.
How do I enroll in a premium course?
To enroll, visit the Premium page, select your desired course (e.g., Yoga, Meditation), and sign up or log in. After enrollment, you’ll gain access to guided sessions tailored to your needs.
What benefits does the Community offer?
Our Community provides a space to connect with like-minded individuals, share experiences, and participate in group discussions or live events, fostering support and motivation.
Can I access courses offline?
Currently, our courses require an internet connection for streaming. We’re working on offline access features for premium users in the future.
How do I contact support?
You can reach our support team via the Contact page or by emailing support@mindfulcheckin.com. We’re available 24/7 to assist with any questions or issues.
